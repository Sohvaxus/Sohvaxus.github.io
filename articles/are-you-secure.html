  1. Introduction

    2. I would like to start this article by saying:

    Security is an illusion.

    Alright, the tone for this article is set. If you thought you were anonymous on the internet, then i've got some bad news for you, and here is why.

    Let's crawl in the head of a regular computer user. You use the internet for basic daily tasks; checking Facebook, online banking and looking up cute cat videos on Google. Now you don't want anyone to know you're looking up cute cat videos because that would damage your reputation as a respected man (given that you are indeed a man). How would you go about avoiding that anyone could ever find out you're looking up cat videos? You open your browser in incognito mode, because this does not save your browser history, cookies, trackers, or temporary files. Is this sufficient to protect you?

    The answer is simple: Not even close.
